Ministries

  • About us

    We are a bible believing church who celebrates God’s presence in worship. We were founded in 1995 and we are excited about what God is doing through our ministry. If you are new, visiting or a long time resident of the Triad area and searching for a place to worship, we extend an invitation to you. We are a small Christian Church in High Point, we welcome everyone regardless of denomination.

  • Vision Statement

    To grow and impact the Haitian community. As a church we are committed to cultivating an involved, passionate and spiritually thriving body of believer.
    “Therefore go and make disciples of all nations, baptizing them in the name of the Father and of the Son and of the Holy Spirit, and teaching them to obey everything I have commanded you. And surely I am with you always, to the very end of the age.” -Mathew 28:19-20(NIV)

  • Mission Statement

    To spread the Gospel not only in our community, but beyond the United States.
